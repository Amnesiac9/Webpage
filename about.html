Mars is a chocolate lab and is just under 4 years old, born in late May. She is a very full of energy dog and loves to play frisbee. She is very friendly and loves to meet new people.

Her favorite toy is a frisbee and she loves to play fetch. She is great at catching the frisbee and will do it all day if you let her.

She is a very good dog and is very smart. She knows how to sit, lay down, shake, wave, play dead, bow, spin, and stand. She is very good at listening and will do anything for a treat.

She can be protective and sometimes barks at people coming into our home, she might seem tough at first, but she could not hurt a fly. She is very sweet and loves to brush up and lean on you, so be careful and don't get nocked over!
